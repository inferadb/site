Your questions, answered

What counts as a check?

Every time your app asks "can user X do action Y on resource Z?" that's one check. Batch requests count as one check per item. Complexity of the underlying policy doesn't affect the count.

What counts as a relationship?

A relationship describes a connection: "alice is an editor of report-q4". We count the total number stored, not writes. Delete a relationship and the count goes down.

What is a vault?

Think of a vault like a database in a database server — a self-contained unit with its own relationships, policies, and audit trail. On Scale plans, each vault is cryptographically isolated from every other.

How does overage billing work?

Each paid plan includes a base allocation of checks and relationships. Go over and you pay the per-unit rate for your plan, billed monthly. No surprises — usage is visible in your dashboard in real time.

What will I actually pay?

Most Launch teams pay $30–60/month. Most Scale teams pay $250–500/month. The platform fee is the floor, not the ceiling — your total depends on check volume and stored relationships. Usage is metered in real time so there are no surprises at the end of the month.

What happens when I hit my free tier limits?

Your app keeps working. Free tier usage degrades gracefully with increased latency rather than hard-cutting requests. You'll receive email notifications as you approach your limits so you can upgrade before it affects your users.

Can I change plans at any time?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. There are no long-term contracts on any self-serve plan.

What is the audit trail?

A tamper-evident log of every authorization decision. On Scale plans, each entry is anchored to a Merkle tree so you can prove the record hasn't been altered.

How is my data secured?

All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). On Scale plans, each vault is cryptographically isolated with its own encryption keys. Audit trail entries are tamper-evident via Merkle proofs. InferaDB Cloud is SOC 2 Type II and GDPR compliant on every plan.

Is the audit trail sufficient for compliance?

Yes. Every authorization decision is logged with full context: who, what, when, and the policy version that produced the decision. On Scale plans, Merkle proofs provide cryptographic evidence that records haven't been altered — suitable for SOC 2, HIPAA, and financial compliance audits.

What if I need to migrate off InferaDB Cloud?

InferaDB has an open source Community Edition (MIT + Apache 2.0) with the same core engine, policy language, and APIs. You can export your relationships and policies at any time and run them on your own infrastructure. No lock-in.

Yes. Apply for up to $10K in credits through our Startup Program. We also offer free Scale-tier access for open source and education projects.
